Cleanliness is Key

Being clean is a good practice to have at all times. However, especially during COVID, there are times where it's even more important. Being clean can be a huge part of keeping yourself and others safe from the virus.

Read on to learn more about the ways being clean can protect you, as well as ways that work best against COVID-19.

How does keeping clean help me?

Keeping clean can protect yourself and others from the virus. One particular way that this is seen is in sanitizing surfaces, uncluding our own bodies. You see, the virus can also spread from the particles that stick on surfaces. By cleaning these, the virus gets eliminated before anyone gets infected. How else can I stay clean and protected?

There are many other ways to protect yourself by being clean. This is a list of some of the most important



  1. Never cough in your hands. This is because you touch everything with them and you might contaminate things.
  2. Don't touch your face since you have a higher chance to get sick if your hands were contaminated
  3. Wash your hands regularly to keep dirt and viruses of them
  4. Disinfect things before you bring them into your house
  5. If coming from outside, try to change clothes before entering your house to keep anything that stuck to your clothes outside.

Summary

Now that you know some ways to be clean and protected, you can better protect yourself from the virus. Note that these are only some of the many things that you can do to be clean and healthy!
